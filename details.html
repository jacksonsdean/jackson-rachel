Some additional info and logistics about the weekend.

Arrival

Check-in on Friday, 7/31 officially begins at 3:00 PM. However, you’re welcome to arrive earlier on Friday if that’s more convenient for you.

Address

17482 O'Neil Rd, Lakeside, MT 59922



Departure

Check-out from the lodge/cabins will be 11 AM on 8/2.

Venue

Spruce Lodge At Flathead Lake

All activities will take place at Spruce Lodge, and parking is available on site. The wedding ceremony will take place on Saturday afternoon, 8/1 at around 4:30.

There is no official dress code. Please wear whatever you feel comfortable and happy in! Daytime temperatures are typically in the low to mid-80s, with evenings cooling into the upper 40s to low 50s.

Feel free to bring a swimsuit if you’d like to take a dip in the lake. There will also be kayaks available on Saturday 8/1, for those that fancy a spin around the lake.

There is cell service on site and Wi-Fi in the lodge.

Food

We’ll be providing the following meals at the lodge:

  • Dinner on 7/31
  • Breakfast, lunch, and dinner on 8/1
  • Breakfast on 8/2.
Keep an eye out for a food preference and restriction questionnaire.

Cabins

There is electricity in the cabins. All bedding will be provided, so you do not need to bring sheets, pillows, or towels. The bathhouses are shared and have restrooms and showers. Please note that the bathhouses do not provide shampoo or conditioner, so bring your own if needed. The lodge will be open and available for everyone to spend time in during the day.
