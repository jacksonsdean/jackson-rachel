A gift to those protecting Montana’s natural beauty is a gift to us. Below are some wonderful nature conservation organizations.

Public lands

Wild Montana

Wild Montana works with policymakers, federal agencies, and local communities to influence public lands management. It champions wilderness designations, sustainable recreation planning, and conservation funding.

Donate to Wild Montana
Flathead Lake

Flathead Lakers

Flathead Lakers leads initiatives to monitor and improve water quality by addressing threats such as nutrient pollution, invasive species, and unregulated shoreline development. It promotes sustainable land-use practices and science-driven policies that safeguard the lake’s clarity and aquatic habitats.

Donate to Flathead Lakers
Freshwater systems

Montana Freshwater Partners

Montana Freshwater Partners’ mission is to sustain healthy freshwater systems. It develops and implements restoration projects such as streambank stabilization, habitat enhancement, and wetland rehabilitation. The organization also facilitates compensatory mitigation and helps communities integrate natural infrastructure into flood resilience planning.

Donate to Montana Freshwater Partners
Land & water

The Nature Conservancy

The Montana chapter has helped conserve more than one million acres of land through easements and acquisitions. Beyond land stewardship, The Nature Conservancy in Montana addresses climate adaptation through renewable energy planning, forest resilience projects, and water management in the face of drought.

Donate to The Nature Conservancy
